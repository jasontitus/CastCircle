CastCircle

The line-learning app for actors. Import your script, rehearse with AI scene partners, and track your progress — all on-device.

Features

  • Import scripts from PDF, text, or Markdown — on-device OCR extracts text from scanned PDFs
  • Intelligent script parsing detects characters, acts, scenes, and stage directions automatically
  • Rehearse with AI-powered text-to-speech that reads your scene partners' lines aloud
  • On-device speech recognition checks your line accuracy in real time
  • Organize productions with cast management and join codes for collaboration
  • Track rehearsal progress across your entire cast
  • 100% on-device AI — your scripts and voice never leave your phone
  • Works offline — no internet required for core features
