Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a script?
Tap "New Production" on the home screen, then choose to import from a PDF, text file, or paste text directly. PDFs are processed using on-device OCR to extract the script text.
What script formats are supported?
CastCircle supports PDF files (via OCR), plain text (.txt), and Markdown (.md) files. The parser works best with scripts in standard format where character names appear in ALL CAPS followed by their dialogue.
Do I need an internet connection?
No. All core features — script import, text-to-speech, speech recognition, and rehearsal — run entirely on your device. An internet connection is only needed if you want to create an account for cloud sync or cast collaboration.
How does rehearsal mode work?
Select your character, then start a rehearsal session. The app reads other characters' lines aloud using text-to-speech, then listens as you speak your lines and checks your accuracy using on-device speech recognition.
How do I invite cast members?
Open your production, go to Cast & Roles, and share the join code with your cast. They can enter the code in the app to join the production and access the script.
Is my data private?
Yes. Scripts and recordings are stored locally on your device by default. Cloud sync is opt-in and requires signing in. All speech processing happens on-device — nothing is sent to external AI services. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How do I delete my data?
You can delete individual productions from the home screen. To delete your account and all cloud data, go to Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.
The app isn't recognizing my lines correctly.
Speech recognition accuracy depends on clear audio input. Try rehearsing in a quiet environment and speaking at a natural pace. The app uses on-device models that improve over time.
