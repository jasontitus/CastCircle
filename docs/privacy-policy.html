CastCircle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Introduction

CastCircle ("we", "our", "the app") is a line-learning tool for actors. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name through our authentication provider (Supabase Auth). Account creation is optional; you may use the app without signing in.

Scripts and Recordings

Scripts you import and audio recordings you make are stored locally on your device. If you sign in and enable cloud sync, this data is uploaded to our servers (hosted on Supabase) so you can access it across devices and share with cast members.

On-Device Processing

CastCircle uses on-device machine learning for:

  • Text-to-speech (TTS): Generating spoken lines using on-device models. No audio data is sent to external servers for this feature.
  • Speech-to-text (STT): Transcribing your spoken lines for accuracy checking. All speech recognition runs locally on your device.
  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition): Extracting text from PDF scripts. Processing happens on-device using ML Kit.

None of these features send your voice, scripts, or personal data to third-party AI services.

Microphone

The app requests microphone access only when you choose to record your lines during rehearsal. Microphone data is processed locally and is never streamed to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the line-learning and rehearsal features of the app
  • To sync your scripts, recordings, and production data across your devices (if you opt in by signing in)
  • To enable cast collaboration features (sharing scripts and tracking rehearsal progress within a production)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • Cast collaboration: If you join a production, other cast members and the organizer can see your display name and rehearsal progress for that production.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Storage and Security

Local data is stored on your device using encrypted storage. Cloud data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies ensuring users can only access their own data and productions they belong to.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your scripts, recordings, and account at any time from within the app. Deleting your account removes all associated cloud data. Local data remains on your device until you delete the app.

Children's Privacy

CastCircle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
