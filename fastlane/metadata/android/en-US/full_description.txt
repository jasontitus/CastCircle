CastCircle is the line-learning app built for actors. Import your script, rehearse with AI scene partners, and track your progress — all powered by on-device AI that keeps your data private.

IMPORT ANY SCRIPT
Import scripts from PDF, plain text, or Markdown. CastCircle uses on-device OCR to extract text from scanned PDFs and intelligently parses characters, acts, scenes, and stage directions automatically.

REHEARSE WITH AI SCENE PARTNERS
Select your character and start rehearsing. CastCircle reads your scene partners' lines aloud using natural text-to-speech, then listens as you speak your lines and checks your accuracy using on-device speech recognition.

COLLABORATE WITH YOUR CAST
Share a join code with your cast so everyone can access the script. Track rehearsal progress across your entire production — see who's learned their lines and who needs more practice.

100% ON-DEVICE AI
All speech processing happens locally on your device. Your scripts, voice recordings, and personal data never leave your phone. No cloud AI services, no data harvesting.

WORKS OFFLINE
Core features work without an internet connection. Import scripts, rehearse, and record — anytime, anywhere. Cloud sync is optional for backing up and sharing across devices.

KEY FEATURES
- PDF import with on-device OCR
- Intelligent script parsing (characters, acts, scenes, stage directions)
- Text-to-speech with configurable voices per character
- Speech-to-text line accuracy checking
- Cast management with join codes
- Rehearsal progress tracking
- Cloud sync (optional)
- Works completely offline
